Casework guidance AI assistant- additional queries

Our system is able to take context from up to 4 queries when generating responses. Therefore, we had to consider how multiple related queries and responses could be displayed.

One way we distinguish between the question and response is the text widths. The users question is displayed at 1/3 width, as it is likely the response will be much longer. In addition to this, the question is on #D2E2F1, whilst the response is on govuk-colour("light-grey") #f3f2f1- these colours have been used on the GOV.UK Chat (experimental). There is a small gap between the boxes to create another visual difference between the question and response.
