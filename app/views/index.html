Casework assistant- white label - prototype

This white label prototype is a generalised version of an AI assistant for caseworkers. This assistant helps users make decisions about applications.

At our organisation, caseworkers have a large library of articles to inform decisions. These articles have been loaded into a Large Language Model (LLM). This prototype is a white label version of the user interface to get responses from the LLM.

We took inspiration and learnings from oour colleagues across government, notably:

  • How we’re designing GOV.UK Chat
  • GOV.UK Chat: Understanding and addressing jailbreaking in our generative AI experiment
  • We’re running a private beta of GOV.UK Chat

Our Assistant is in private beta with staff. To share our thinking and design decisions about AI UI, we've removed the real content and replaced it with fictional information - so the design can be seen.

You can contact us on X-Gov slack: Andy Porter (Lead Interaction Designer), Romani Davies (Interaction Designer), Kit Harding (Content Designer) and Tjingmi Chow (Senior User Researcher).
